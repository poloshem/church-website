What We Believe

Doctrinal Statement of Life Spring Dominion Church International

Statement of Faith

We affirm that the Holy Bible is the inspired, infallible, and final authority in all matters of faith and conduct.

The Godhead

We believe in one God existing eternally in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

Jesus Christ is the Son of God, fully divine and fully human, born of a virgin, and the only Savior of mankind.

Sin and Salvation

All humanity is sinful, and salvation is only through repentance and faith in Jesus Christ.
