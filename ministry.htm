Our Ministries

🎤 Worship & Praise Team

Lead our congregation in worship through music, vocals, and instrumentation. Practice sessions held weekly.

👶 Children's Ministry

Teach, nurture, and disciple our children through engaging Bible lessons, crafts, and activities.

🍽️ Hospitality & Ushers

Welcome visitors, assist with seating, manage refreshments, and ensure everyone feels at home.

📖 Bible Teaching & Discipleship

Share God's word through teaching, preaching, and one-on-one discipleship opportunities.

💪 Men's & Women's Fellowship

Lead gender-specific groups focused on spiritual growth, accountability, and fellowship.

🎭 Drama & Creative Arts

Use drama, dance, and creative expressions to communicate God's message in powerful ways.

💰 Finance & Administration

Help manage church finances, record keeping, and administrative support functions.

Participate in local and international missions, community service, and evangelism programs.
