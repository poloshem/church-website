Who We Are

Learn more about Dominion Spring International Church and our vision.

Our Vision

“To raise a global generation of Spirit-filled believers overflowing with the life of Christ - transforming nations through the rivers of living waters that flow within them, bringing healing, revival and dominion to every sphere of life.”
(Referenced scripture: John 7:38)

Our Mission

“To lead people into a deep, life-giving relationship with Jesus Christ, nurturing them through the true Word and the Spirit until they become fountains of divine life — impacting their communities, discipling nations and manifesting God’s Kingdom, power and glory in every place.”
(Referenced scripture: John 14:6)

Our Core Values

  • Faith in God: We live by unwavering faith in the Word and promises of God.
  • The Word and the Spirit: We are guided and empowered by the living Word and the Holy Spirit.
  • Holiness and Integrity: We walk in purity, truth, and righteousness.
  • Love and Compassion: We demonstrate Christ’s love through service and care for others.
  • Excellence and Stewardship: We serve God with diligence, creativity, and excellence.
  • Discipleship and Dominion: We raise Kingdom leaders who influence and transform society.
  • Revival and Impact: We carry the life of the Spirit to bring revival and transformation.

Who We Are

Dominion Spring International Church is a vibrant and growing community of believers committed to sharing the love of Christ. We are passionate about building lives through sound teaching, fellowship, and outreach initiatives that impact our society.

Our Community

Our church is more than a place of worship—it is a family. We foster meaningful relationships through small groups, youth programs, and community outreach activities that bring people together in unity and purpose.
