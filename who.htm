Who We Are

Learn more about Dominion Spring International Church and our vision.

Our Vision

“To raise a global generation of Spirit-filled believers overflowing with the life of Christ...”
(John 7:38)

Our Mission

“To lead people into a deep, life-giving relationship with Jesus Christ...”
(John 14:6)

Our Core Values

  • Faith in God: We live by unwavering faith in the Word of God.
  • The Word and the Spirit: Guided by Scripture and the Holy Spirit.
  • Holiness and Integrity: We walk in purity and righteousness.
  • Love and Compassion: We serve others in Christ’s love.
  • Excellence and Stewardship: We serve with diligence and excellence.
  • Discipleship and Dominion: We raise Kingdom leaders.
  • Revival and Impact: We bring transformation through the Spirit.

Who We Are

Dominion Spring International Church is a vibrant and growing community of believers committed to sharing the love of Christ.

Our Community

Our church is more than a place of worship—it is a family built on fellowship, outreach, and unity.
